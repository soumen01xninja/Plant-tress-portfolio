Watering: Needs consistent moisture. Water deeply when the topsoil feels slightly dry.

Sunlight: Requires bright, indirect sunlight. Direct noon sun can scorch the miniature leaves.

Pruning: Regular trimming is essential to maintain the desired structural canopy.
